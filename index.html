About Me

Hi, I'm Randy aka Thrax or Thraxsquad. I own a 2022 Tesla Model 3 Performance in Orlando Florida. I've always been passionate about cars, and I'm grateful I'm at that point in life where I'm able to build something that I think is truly unique and different from what you currently see driving around.

Besides just working on my car, attending car meets and car shows I also own and run Electrified Car Club which is a car club for exclusively electric vehicles. We host meets, rallies, shows, and also host events to give back to the community, which ultimately is my favorite part.
